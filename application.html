Overview

Asteroids is a classic arcade-style space shooter where you control a spaceship navigating through an asteroid field. The goal is to destroy incoming asteroids and enemy saucers while avoiding collisions. As you progress, the number and speed of asteroids increase, making the game more challenging. Test your reflexes and piloting skills in this timeless space adventure!

How To Play

  1. Once the game loads in the frame above, you'll see your spaceship in the vast expanse of space.
  2. Asteroids of various sizes will begin to drift across the screen. Your mission is to destroy them before they collide with your ship.
  3. You'll also encounter enemy saucers that will periodically appear and try to shoot you down. Be quick and precise with your targeting!
  4. When you destroy a large asteroid, it will break into several smaller asteroids, which are faster and harder to hit.
  5. Survive for as long as possible and aim for the highest score!
