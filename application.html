Game Overview - What's the Rush?

Food Rush puts you in the shoes of a busy restaurant worker! You'll need lightning-fast reflexes and top-notch multitasking skills to keep your hungry customers happy. Serve up their orders quickly and efficiently to earn big points and keep the rush going!

How to Play - Get Serving!

  1. Keep your eyes peeled on the customer orders popping up! They're not waiting!
  2. Quickly identify the food items each customer wants. Time is money!
  3. Use your mouse to click and drag the correct food items from the preparation stations to the waiting customers. Serve 'em right!
  4. Watch out for multiple orders at once! You'll need to prioritize and be efficient to handle the growing demand.
  5. Rack up points by serving customers quickly and accurately. The faster you are, the bigger your score!
  6. Don't let your customers get too impatient, or they'll leave, and you'll lose points! Keep that service speed high!
  7. Try to complete each level by reaching the target score before time runs out. It's a food frenzy!

Game Controls - Master the Moves!

It's all about the clicks and drags! Here's how you control the Food Rush:

  • Mouse Click: Use the left mouse button to select food items from the preparation areas.
  • Drag and Drop: Click and hold the selected food item, then drag it to the corresponding customer to serve their order. Release the mouse button to deliver the food.
  • Eye Coordination: Keep a sharp eye on the orders and the food stations. Quick recognition is key!
  • Speed and Precision: Fast and accurate movements with your mouse will lead to higher scores and happier customers!

Get those clicks ready and start serving up a storm!
